Chocolate Glazed Donuts

Rating

Notes

I love donuts. So much. Especially chocolate glazed donuts. Those are the best. This joshua weismann recipe was great. The only reason I knocked it down a peg is that donuts are a lot of work, and I can be a bit of a perfectionist. Lucky enough to live close to a good donut shop and I would probably stick to that. It was worth trying to see how the donuts were made though!

Bomboloni

Rating

Notes

Everytime we visit my family in Italy, I make sure to get bomboloni. These nutella filled donuts are the best. So much so, I don't understand why donut shops here don't make them. I love them so much, I made sure to have them the morning of my wedding. These are worth the make, if for no other reason that you can't really find them here and they are fried, nutella, pillowy goodness. Joshua Weismann did not let us down with this recipe.

Focaccia

Rating

Notes

Focaccia makes for a good sandwich or a standalone side. I've done this recipe with a classing rosemary salt topping, olives and last but not least a thinly sliced potato. All of those toppings are good and this Bon Appetit focaccia recipe does a nice job of making a conistent airy focaccia.

Sourdough

Rating

Notes

You know how sourdough was all the craze during the pandemic. Well we hopped on that train too and tried to make our own sourdough. This recipe actually worked well. The main thing was it was really difficult to keep up with the starter and consistently make it. Worth it if you enjoy the idea of taking care of a "pet" and making bread every week with it. The Perfect Loaf blog was a great place to learn all about making sourdough.
