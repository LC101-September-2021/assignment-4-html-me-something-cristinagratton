Suppli

Rating

Notes

My parents immigrated to the US from close to Rome, where these are extremely popular treats to get at the pizza shop. Impossible to find the exact combination of fried tomato mozzarella risotto goodness in the US, we found this recipe to make them at home. They turn out so yummy. There has been some trial and error in terms of size and ratio of risotto to mozarella so it properly melts on the inside, but they are so worth the time and energy. Make some extra that you can freeze, and finish in the future and you won't regret it!
