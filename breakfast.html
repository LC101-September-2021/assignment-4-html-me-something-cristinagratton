Banana Bread

Rating

Notes

Anything that is titled best entices you to try it. This was an early recipe made during the pandemic--entitled BA's Best Banana Bread. It uses mascarpone to make the batter which is mixed until its light and fluffy. This was a very good banana bread and I would recommend this recipe. It went well toasted with butter and some flaky salt. I left out the optional nuts and chocolate chips as I like a classic banana bread a little more

Cinnamon Babka

Rating

Notes

During the early days of the pandemic we discovered Joshua Weismann on youtube. After watching his babka video it felt like a must try. We didn't have two loaf pans so instead we made it into a longer braid and used a cake pan. This was absolutely delicious, very fluffy. We did a cinnamon stuffing, and have retried it since with Nutella which was also very good.

Savory Dutch Baby

Rating

Notes

This Claire Saffitz Bon Appetit recipe is great as a leftover/empty the fridge day. It is very straightforward to make and can have lots of different toppings. We doubled the batch and used a larger cast iron to make it a heartier meal. Make sure to properly aerate your eggs or it won't inflate right in the oven. We used lox, capers and cream cheese as toppings on one half. And a prosciutto, cheese topping on the other.

Notes
